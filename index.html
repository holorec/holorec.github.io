Hiking the Grand Staircae

Videos from the trip

In July 2018, a group of us from Packard Bell Children's Hospital (Kathy Ho, Dave Berry) and the University of Cambridge Computer Laboratory (Gemma Gordon, Anil Madhavapeddy) began a project to experiment with the use of Virtual Reality technology to aid in the teaching of science.  We wanted to focus on the topic of the human impact on the environment, and so started with the US National Parks in the Grand Staircase region of the United States.   We are now processing the data, and this page contains some sample videos as we experiment with generating virtual reality content for courses in LabSci.
